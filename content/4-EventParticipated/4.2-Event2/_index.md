### General Information

### Event Introduction
The event on July 11th consisted of two main parts: a thrilling Cloud Architect final competition and 3 insightful presentations from 3 guest speakers. It was a great opportunity to learn about practical AWS architectures, application security, and valuable tips for certification exams.

### Key Takeaways for Myself and the Project

After the event, I gained several valuable lessons:
* **Immediate Project Application:** Learned basic security setups and end-user experience monitoring mindset instead of just tracking CPU/Memory.
* **Professional Soft Skills:** Felt the intensity of the final competition, which motivated me to improve my personal skills and work more effectively in a team.
* **Expanding Community Connections:** Had the opportunity to learn from experienced professionals and better understand the roadmap to achieving the AWS Cloud Practitioner certification.
