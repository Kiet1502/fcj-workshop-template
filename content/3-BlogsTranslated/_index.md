This section showcases the **3 practical technical blogs** authored, published, and shared by our team to the **AWS Study Group Vietnam** community during our First Cloud Journey (FCJ) internship and while building **Aura Academic | Smart Exam Engine**:

---

### [Blog 1 - Building an AI-Powered Online Examination Platform on AWS Cloud (Serverless Architecture)](3.1-Blog1/)
* **Original Facebook Post:** [Read on AWS Study Vietnam Community](https://www.facebook.com/share/p/1D6dVxB4R3/?)  
* **Abstract:** Details the architecture and operational journey of developing **Aura Academic | Smart Exam Engine**. Focuses on applying Cloud-Native and Serverless paradigms for maximum cost optimization and low latency: leveraging **Amazon S3** and **Amazon CloudFront** for high-speed static frontend hosting, along with **AWS Lambda**, **Amazon API Gateway**, and **Amazon DynamoDB** to process thousands of concurrent exam submissions without managing dedicated infrastructure (**Pay-as-you-go**).

---

### [Blog 2 - Integrating Artificial Intelligence (Generative AI & Computer Vision) for Exam Supervision](3.2-Blog2/)
* **Original Facebook Post:** [Read on AWS Study Group Community](https://www.facebook.com/photo/?fbid=1676460976896951&set=gm.2201043733993920&idorvanity=660548818043427)  
* **Abstract:** A deep dive into integrating AWS AI/ML services into a real-world EdTech product. Explores how **Amazon Bedrock** (LLMs) powers the **Exam Builder** to automatically parse Word/PDF documents into multi-difficulty question matrices in seconds, and how **Amazon Rekognition** (Computer Vision) enables real-time **AI Proctoring** to detect multi-face presence, identity swapping, and unauthorized behavior inside **Secure Rooms**.

---

### [Blog 3 - Automating CI/CD Pipelines, CloudWatch Monitoring & Zero-Trust Security on AWS](3.3-Blog3/)
* **Original Facebook Post:** [Read on AWS Study Vietnam Community](https://www.facebook.com/share/p/18uKARgWds/?)  
* **Abstract:** Synthesizes hands-on lab experiences and engineering best practices from our First Cloud Journey (FCJ) program. Step-by-step guide on setting up automated continuous integration and delivery pipelines using **AWS CodePipeline & CodeBuild**, configuring **AWS WAF** and **Amazon Route 53** to defend APIs against OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities, and implementing real-time infrastructure monitoring with **Amazon CloudWatch Dashboards/Alarms** and **Amazon SNS**.
