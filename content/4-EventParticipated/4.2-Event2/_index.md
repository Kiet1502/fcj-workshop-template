# Event: Cloud Architect Final & Seminar

### General Information
- **Event Name:** Cloud Architect Final & Seminar
- **Date:** Saturday, July 11, 2026
- **Role:** Attendee

---

### Event Introduction
The event on July 11th consisted of two main parts: a thrilling Cloud Architect final competition and 3 insightful presentations from 3 guest speakers. It was a great opportunity to learn about practical AWS architectures, application security, and valuable tips for certification exams.
